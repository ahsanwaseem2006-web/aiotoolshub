Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and handle your information.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At AIO Tools Hub, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and tools.

Last Updated: October 15, 2023

Information We Collect

We designed our tools with privacy as a core principle. Most of our tools process your data entirely in your browser, meaning your information never leaves your device.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how our tools are being used and to improve user experience. This includes information like which tools are most popular, average session duration, and general geographic location (country-level).
  • Technical Information: We may collect browser type, version, and device type to ensure our tools work correctly across different platforms.
  • Cookies: We use minimal cookies to remember your preferences and improve site functionality.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To improve our tools and user experience
  • To understand how our tools are being used
  • To fix bugs and technical issues
  • To develop new tools based on user needs
  • To ensure the security and proper functioning of our website

Data Processing in Our Tools

One of our core privacy principles is that your data should remain under your control. For most of our tools:

  • Files you upload (images, documents, etc.) are processed entirely in your browser
  • Your data is never transmitted to our servers
  • We don't store your files or the content you process through our tools
  • Once you close your browser tab, all processed data is cleared

The only exceptions are tools that inherently require server processing (like URL shortening), and these are clearly indicated.

Third-Party Services

We use a few trusted third-party services to help operate our website:

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site. This information is anonymous and aggregated.
  • Hosting: Our website is hosted on secure servers with reputable providers.

These services have their own privacy policies, and we ensure they meet our standards for data protection.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to processing of your personal information
  • Request transfer of your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in our Contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: privacy@aiotoolshub.com
  • Through our contact form on the Contact page
